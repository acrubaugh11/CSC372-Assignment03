Don

Don is a Japanese restuarant located on Tate Street with food ranging from around $10-$20 USD. It's hours are usually 11am - 3:30pm and 5-10 pm.

Bulgogi Don

Bulgogi is a popular korean dish of thinly sliced meat.

Spicy Ramen

Japanese dish featuring spicy, creamy, rich ramen noodles with meat.

Spicy Tuna

A dish served with spicy tuna and rice.

Slices Pizza by Tony

Slices Pizza by Tony is a small pizza shop located on Tate Street and holds many famous local Italin dishes like Zeppoli! It's open regularly from 10:30 am to 10pm.

Zeppoli

Zeppoli is a famous Italian pastry, consisting of deep-fried dough ball.

Calzone

Calzone is an oven-baked Italian folded pizza, with options for meat, veggies and other toppings inside!

Pizza

Last but not least, classic Italian pizza! And no better place to get it than at Tony's!

Raaz Indian Kitchen

Raaz Indian Kitchen, located on Tate Street features many Indian/Pakistani dishes. It is open from 11:30 am to 10 pm regularly.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a classic curried dish with soft tender marinated chicken pieces and rice usually.

Garlic Naan

Garlic Naan is a flavorful leavend Idian flatbread, baked in a clay oven!

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is an aroumatic, layered rice dish from India/Pakistan with chicken marinated with lots of spices and rice.
